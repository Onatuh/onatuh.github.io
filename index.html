About

"Hi, I'm Lee. I am British Weightlifting certified and the Head coach of the Chichester Weightlifting Club (CWC). I also have a BWL Strength & Power coach qualification as well as a L4 Strength & Conditioning coach qualification.

I started the weightlifting club because of my fascination of all things weightlifting and the desire to help people move better.
We train the technical aspects of both lifts as well compound strength, power movements, unilateral stability and mobility.
I believe in proper movement before weight and having a little fun along the way."
